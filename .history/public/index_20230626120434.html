My projects:

The Cookbook website allows users to submit their recipes, providing details such as the recipe name, cooking and preparation time, and an accompanying picture. It serves as a virtual collection of diverse culinary creations, enabling users to share their favorite dishes with others. Whether you're seeking inspiration or eager to contribute your own recipe, the Cookbook website offers a platform for food enthusiasts to discover and showcase their culinary expertise.

The Random Quote Generator website offers users a unique experience by utilizing an API to display a variety of thought-provoking quotes. With each refresh, users are presented with a new quote, allowing them to explore different perspectives and ideas. When users come across a quote that resonates with them, they have the option to easily share it on Twitter, spreading inspiration and sparking conversations.

The Task List website offers users a convenient way to keep track of their tasks by providing an intuitive interface for inputting and managing tasks. Leveraging the power of local storage, users can safely store their tasks even after refreshing the page, ensuring their progress is never lost. This seamless implementation of local storage adds a practical and enjoyable aspect to the task management process, allowing users to focus on their to-do list without worrying about data loss.

The Neat Calculator website offers users a visually appealing and user-friendly calculator interface. With its sleek design, users can perform various mathematical operations with ease and precision. From simple arithmetic calculations to more complex equations, the Neat Calculator provides a convenient tool for users to crunch numbers efficiently and accurately.

Introducing "Content Showcase," a dynamic boilerplate website designed specifically for potential clients in need of a static site to display their content.
